# Third-party fonts

CRSScheduler bundles two open-source fonts under `app/src/main/res/font/`.
Both are licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1 (OFL-1.1), copied in
full in their respective subfolders here.

| Font file(s) | Family | Authors | License |
|---|---|---|---|
| `jetbrains_mono.ttf`, `jetbrains_mono_bold.ttf` | [JetBrains Mono](https://github.com/JetBrains/JetBrainsMono) | The JetBrains Mono Project Authors | [OFL-1.1](jetbrains-mono/OFL.txt) |
| `dotgothic16.ttf` | [DotGothic16](https://github.com/fontworks-fonts/DotGothic16) | The DotGothic16 Project Authors (Fontworks Inc.) | [OFL-1.1](dotgothic16/OFL.txt) |

The OFL permits bundling these fonts with an app free of charge, which is
how they're used here (as `.ttf` resources, not sold or redistributed on
their own). No source code changes were made to either font.
